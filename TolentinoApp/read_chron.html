I Chronicles

I Chron:1:1: Adam, Sheth, Enosh,
I Chron:1:2: Kenan, Mahalaleel, Jered,
I Chron:1:3: Henoch, Methuselah, Lamech,
I Chron:1:4: Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.
I Chron:1:5: The sons of Japheth; Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.
I Chron:1:6: And the sons of Gomer; Ashchenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.
I Chron:1:7: And the sons of Javan; Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.
I Chron:1:8: The sons of Ham; Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.
I Chron:1:9: And the sons of Cush; Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raamah, and Sabtecha. And the sons of Raamah; Sheba, and Dedan.
I Chron:1:10: And Cush begat Nimrod: he began to be mighty upon the earth.
I Chron:1:11: And Mizraim begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,
I Chron:1:12: And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, (of whom came the Philistines,) and Caphthorim.
I Chron:1:13: And Canaan begat Zidon his firstborn, and Heth,
I Chron:1:14: The Jebusite also, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite,
I Chron:1:15: And the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,
I Chron:1:16: And the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.
I Chron:1:17: The sons of Shem; Elam, and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech.
I Chron:1:18: And Arphaxad begat Shelah, and Shelah begat Eber.
I Chron:1:19: And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of the one was Peleg; because in his days the earth was divided: and his brother's name was Joktan.
I Chron:1:20: And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,
I Chron:1:21: Hadoram also, and Uzal, and Diklah,
I Chron:1:22: And Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba,
I Chron:1:23: And Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.
